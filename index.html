Crystals Collector



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By click on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number provided. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number provided.
The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
