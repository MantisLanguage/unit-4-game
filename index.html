Yoshi's Story



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four eggs below. By clicking on an egg you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number provided.
You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number provided.
The value of each egg will be hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each egg.
